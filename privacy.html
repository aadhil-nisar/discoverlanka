Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to DiscoverLanka

This Privacy Policy explains, in a simple way, how this student project may collect and use information shared through the website.

Privacy Policy

DiscoverLanka is currently a university student project and is still under development. This website may collect limited information that users enter voluntarily through forms, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, reviews, and messages.

1. Information You Provide

When you use contact forms, newsletter forms, review forms, or planning tools, you may choose to provide personal information. This information is only collected through what you enter yourself.

2. How Information May Be Used

Information submitted through the website may be used for project demonstration purposes, user communication, feedback review, website improvement, and academic presentation of the project.

3. Student Project Notice

DiscoverLanka is not yet a fully launched commercial platform. Because this is a project in development, data handling methods may change as the system continues to improve.

4. Reviews and User Content

If you submit reviews, comments, or other user content, that content may be displayed on the website as part of the project experience.

5. Third-Party Content

Some design assets, icons, fonts, or demo images may come from third-party services or public sources. Their own privacy and usage terms may apply where relevant.

6. Data Security

Reasonable care is intended in project design, but because this is still a student development project, complete production-level security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the website grows and features change.

8. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions about DiscoverLanka, please contact us through the Contact page.
