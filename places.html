Five Main Categories

Explore Sri Lanka your way by selecting the kind of destinations you want to experience

Beaches

Pristine coastlines with golden sands and crystal-clear waters perfect for relaxation

Sri Lanka is surrounded by beautiful beaches that offer a wide variety of travel experiences. Some beaches are perfect for swimming and relaxing, while others are famous for surfing, whale watching, snorkeling, sunsets, seafood, and vibrant beachside stays.

Southern and western coastal destinations are popular for lively beach holidays, while eastern beaches provide seasonal calm waters and more peaceful seaside escapes.

Relaxing coastal towns Surf and sunset spots Whale watching areas
Explore Beaches

Hill Country

Lush tea plantations and misty mountains offering breathtaking scenic views

Sri Lanka’s hill country is one of the most scenic regions on the island, known for cool weather, tea estates, winding roads, waterfalls, viewpoints, train journeys, forests, and peaceful mountain towns.

Destinations in this category are ideal for travelers who love fresh air, panoramic landscapes, hiking, photography, and slower journeys through beautiful upland environments.

Tea country landscapes Cool weather escapes Viewpoints and train routes
Explore Hill Country

Waterfalls

Magnificent cascades and hidden falls surrounded by forests, mountains, and scenic valleys

Sri Lanka has many spectacular waterfalls spread across the central highlands and nearby regions. Some are easy roadside stops, while others are reached through walking paths, tea estates, forest trails, and scenic mountain roads.

This category is perfect for travelers who enjoy natural beauty, photography, adventure, and dramatic landscapes shaped by flowing water and rocky terrain.

Mountain waterfalls Scenic road trip stops Nature photography spots
Explore Waterfalls

Heritage Sites

Ancient temples and UNESCO World Heritage sites rich in cultural history

Sri Lanka’s heritage sites reflect a long and remarkable history shaped by ancient kingdoms, religion, art, architecture, and cultural tradition. These destinations include sacred temples, historic cities, rock fortresses, cave shrines, and colonial landmarks.

This category is ideal for travelers who want to explore the cultural depth of Sri Lanka and experience places of historical, spiritual, and architectural importance.

Sacred temples and shrines Ancient cities and ruins UNESCO heritage landmarks
Explore Heritage

Wildlife

National parks teeming with elephants, leopards, and exotic bird species

Sri Lanka is one of the best wildlife destinations in Asia, offering national parks, forests, wetlands, safari zones, and biodiversity-rich habitats filled with fascinating species. Travelers can experience elephants, leopards, birds, deer, crocodiles, and many more animals.

This category is best for nature lovers, safari travelers, birdwatchers, and anyone who wants to experience the island’s wild side through protected natural environments.

National park safaris Elephant and leopard habitats Birdlife and rainforest zones
